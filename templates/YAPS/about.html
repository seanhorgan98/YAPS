About YAPS

YAPS (Yet Another Podcast Site) is a website dedicated to creating a community which allows users to create, listen and enjoy podcasts of any genre. A goal of ours is to let users browse new or old podcasts with the ability to save/favourite or to rate and comment on podcasts they really like or really dislike.



Our team consists of Andrew, Jim, Jeff and Sean.
